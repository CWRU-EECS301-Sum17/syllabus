### SCPI Trigger Model

* Basis for most Instrumentation trigger systems
* SCPI Trigger Model Layers
	* Idle
	* Initiate
	* Arm
	* Trigger
	* Device Action
* http://www.ivifoundation.org/docs/scpi-99.pdf

Note:

IVI Foundation Standard
